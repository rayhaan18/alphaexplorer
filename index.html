Strategy: Asymmetric Bets

Asymmetric bets mean investing in opportunities where the potential upside far outweighs the risk. You want to find bets that can multiply your money, even if many fail.

Strategy: Contrarian Investing

Contrarian investing is about going against the crowd. When others sell in panic, contrarians buy. When the market is overly optimistic, contrarians sell. This approach can help capture alpha during market swings.
